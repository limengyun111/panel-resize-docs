#### 代码示例

此示例展示了嵌套组，可左右/上下拖拽调整面板大小。

<NestedDemo />

```jsx
<PanelGroup direction="horizontal">
  <Panel>
    left
  </Panel>
  <PanelResizeHandle />
  <Panel>
    <PanelGroup direction="vertical">
      <Panel>
        top
      </Panel>
      <PanelResizeHandle />
      <Panel>
        <PanelGroup direction="horizontal">
          <Panel>
            left
          </Panel>
          <PanelResizeHandle />
          <Panel>
            right
          </Panel>
        </PanelGroup>
      </Panel>
    </PanelGroup>
  </Panel>
  <PanelResizeHandle />
  <Panel>
    right
  </Panel>
</PanelGroup>
```
